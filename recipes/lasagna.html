Lasagna

Description

Oh to be young and full of hope.

A mentor of mine once told me that his muse was an orange tabby cat whose palate was so refined that only the finest of pasta cakes could sate his appetite. This is that recipe. Yeet yah.

Ingredients

  • pasta sheet
  • tomato sauce
  • ricotta cheese
  • basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta sheets in heavily salted water for 5 to 6 minutes until al dente.
  2. Remove the sheets of pasta and immediately lay dry on paper towels. CAREFUL don't break the sheets.
  3. Begin layering your sheets of pasta and sauce in a large Dollar Tree aluminum baking tray
  4. Remember to alternate between pasta sauces and ricotta cheese
  5. Bake your lasagna at 375 degrees fahrenheit for 1 hour
  6. Remove from oven and let rest for up to an hour
  7. Garnish with basil leaves and serve!
